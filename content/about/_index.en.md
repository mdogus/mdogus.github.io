Mustafa Doğuş is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Special Education, Faculty of Education, at Anadolu University. His academic work centers on the education of individuals with visual impairment, Braille literacy and instruction, accessible testing environments, and the role of assistive technologies in special education.

Across publications and conference papers, his research consistently examines how accessibility can be designed within both classroom practice and assessment settings. In recent years, this line of work has also expanded toward the relationship between visual impairment and artificial intelligence.
